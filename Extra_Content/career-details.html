At Corva, we are looking for a highly analytical, results-driven Business Consultant who has a strong understanding of modern business strategy. The core objective is to deliver optimizing operations and growth strategies with real-world impact. If you’re passionate about problem-solving, strategic thinking, and delivering measurable business value, we want to hear from you.

What You’ll Do:

  • Analyze business processes and develop strategies to improve performance and efficiency
  • Create data-driven insights and actionable plans based on market trends and research
  • Apply strong analytical thinking, problem solving, and strategic planning techniques
  • Collaborate with clients, stakeholders, and internal teams to deliver solutions
  • Ensure project success through consistent execution and performance tracking
  • Stay updated with industry trends, business tools, and consulting best practices

What We’re Looking For:

  • A strong portfolio showcasing business strategy, consulting cases, and analysis work
  • Excellent understanding of market trends, data analysis, and business growth strategies
  • Apply strong analytical thinking, communication, and decision-making skills
  • Proficiency in Excel, analytics tools, or business intelligence platforms
  • Deep understanding of business models, operations, and consulting frameworks
  • Experience working with diverse industries and scalable business solutions
  • Team player with strong communication and client management skills
  • Bonus: Experience with startups, digital transformation, or growth consulting

Why Join Corva:

  • Opportunity to work on impactful business transformation projects.
  • Grow your skills in a dynamic, innovative, and collaborative environment.
  • Competitive salary, incentives, and professional development support.
